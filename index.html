Company Profile

Our Vision

To be the foremost indigenous ship management company in Nigeria, recognized for delivering world-class technical and operational excellence, built on Nigerian expertise and trusted on a global stage.

Our Mission

To draw on the ABC Group's 43-year legacy in offshore and tanker vessel technical management to deliver ship management services that consistently meet the highest international and oil major standards, while strengthening local capability, developing Nigerian talent, and creating long-term value within Nigeria.

About NBC Maritime

NBC Maritime is a premier ship management company delivering world-class maritime operations across West Africa, fully compliant with Cabotage, NIMASA, CVFF, and NOGICD regulations.

Office in Apapa, Lagos, we combine European technical discipline with strong local leadership to provide safe, efficient, and reliable ship management solutions. Our services support shipowners, financiers, and charterers with comprehensive technical, operational, and regulatory assurance.
